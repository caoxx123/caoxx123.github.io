Bend your universe

Our thoughts feeling.dreams.and ideas are physical in the Universe.

If we dream something ,if we picture something ,and if we commit ourselves to it-that is a physical thrust towards realization that we send out into the universe.

The universe it not a thing that’s going to push us around.

The world.people nd sitations are not going to push us around.

We are going to bend the universe;

we are going to command and DEMAND that the universe become what we want it to be.

I studied the patterns of the universe.

There’s a redemptive power that making a choice has.

Rather than feeling like you’re just addected bu all the things happening make a choice

Just DECIDE what you’re gonna be.who you’re gonna be and how you’re gonna do it.

Just decide

From that point on,the Universe is gonna get out your way.
