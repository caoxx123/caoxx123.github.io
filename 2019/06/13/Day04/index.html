Good artists copy, Great artists steal

How do you know what’s the right direction?

Uitimately it comes down to taste.

It’s comes down to taste.

It’s a matter of trying to express yourself to the best things that humans has done.

And then trying to bring those things into what you are doing.

Picasso had a saying:”Good artists copy,Greate artist steal”.

We has always been shameless about stealling great ideas.
