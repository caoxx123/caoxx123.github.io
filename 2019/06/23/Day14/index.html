Sickening work ethic

The separation of talent and skill is one of the greatest misunderstood concepts for people who are frying to excel who have derams,Who want to do things.

Talent you have naturally.

Skill is only developed by hours and hours of beating on your craft.

I’ve never really viewed myself as particularly talented.

And where I exeel is ridiculous .sickening work ethic.

You know,while the other guy’s sleeping ? I’m working

While the other guy’s eatin? I’m working.

There’s no easy way around it.

No matter how talented you are,you talent is going to fail you if you’re not skilled.

If you don’t study work really hard and dedicate yourslef to getting better every single day.

You’ll never be able to communicate you artistry to people the way that you want.

The only thing that I see that is distinctly different about me that is that I’m not afarid to die on a treadmill.

You might have more tablent than me ,you minght be smarter than me ,but if we get on a treadmill togerther there’re to two things.

You’re getting off first.or I’m gonna die.

It’s really that simple.
